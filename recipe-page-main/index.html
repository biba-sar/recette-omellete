Simple Omelette Recipe

A easy and quick dish,perfct for any meal.this classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection,optionnaly filled with your choices of cheese.vegetable or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10miutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salte, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespon of butter or oils
  • Optional fillings cheese, dices vegetables,cooked meat, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffler texture.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, for in the eggs. Tilt the
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add filling (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are stll slightly
    runny in the middle, sprinckle yor chosen fillings over one half of the omelette
  5. Fold and serve: As the omelette continue to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the filling. let it cooked another minutes, then slide anta a plate
  6. Enjoy: serve hot ,with additional salt and Pepper if needed.
